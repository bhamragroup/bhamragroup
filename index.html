Bhamra Concrete Private Limited

Bhamra Concrete Private Limited, a trusted name in the concrete transportation industry, boasts a formidable fleet of over 250 Transit Mixers. Our commitment to excellence is evident as we deliver Ready Mix Concrete seamlessly across the entire expanse of Mumbai. With a market share exceeding a quarter, we proudly stand as a key player in RMC Logistics in the Mumbai region. Our expansive fleet and market dominance underscore our dedication to reliable and efficient concrete delivery, making us the preferred choice for projects in this dynamic urban landscape.

Fuelmen Services Private Limited

Fuelmen Services Private Limited is a pioneer in doorstep diesel delivery, providing an innovative solution to meet your fuel needs. Our mobile fuel tanker fleet ensures that diesel is conveniently delivered right to your doorstep. With a robust operational model, we consistently sell an impressive two lakhs litres of diesel monthly, catering to the diverse fuel requirements of our valued customers. Fuelmen Services is your reliable partner for efficient and hassle-free fuel supply, committed to enhancing convenience in every drop.
